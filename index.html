Why
Conversations?

As society seems more hostile to the Christian worldview, it is critical that believers remember that God used ordinary people in ordinary conversations to transform the world.

That means Christians need to learn again how to talk to the people around us — and show them how good the gospel really is.

What we do

What is Redeeming Conversations?

A Christian apologetics ministry equipping believers to talk to their friends and family. We provide the skills and strategies to engage those around us as winsome ambassadors for Christ — turning hard topics like sexuality, gender, and biblical truth into fruitful engagements.

Conversation Training

Our speakers can present at your church or ministry event. Whether a Sunday morning sermon or a formal university debate, they speak on topics that help your congregation and supporters better engage their friends and family on spiritual truths.

Ministry Consulting

Whether it's conversations on God, the Bible, or LGBTQ questions, Christians have great answers to share. We help ministries across the country write policies, choose communications strategies, and learn to talk with their neighbours again about the truths that matter.

Meet the team

Our Speakers

Available for your church, conference, debate, classroom, or media event.

Jojo Ruba

Executive Director

A Christian apologist from Calgary, Alberta, Jojo explains the Christian faith in ways non-Christians can understand and be challenged by. With over 20 years of experience in apologetics, Christian communications, and public policy, he specializes in helping believers engage in thoughtful conversations with their friends, neighbours, and the public. He also serves as a youth pastor.

Jocelynn Rodrigues

Speaker & Evangelist

Jocelynn articulately speaks truth into the darkness, seeking to dispel the lies that keep people from Jesus. Having once dealt with her own gender confusion, she now shares how she was radically transformed by the love of Christ. She has an evangelistic heart for the LGBTQ community and those hurt by the trauma of abortion — pointing people to the healing available in Christ, knowing it is His kindness that leads to repentance. She speaks one-on-one, or in small or large groups, both in a formal and casual capacity.
